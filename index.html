How is this different from hiring an immigration consultant?

Our coaching program empowers you to handle your own application while providing expert guidance. You save thousands in consultant fees while maintaining full control of your process.

What if I'm not eligible for PR?

We'll assess your eligibility in the first session and provide honest feedback. If you're not currently eligible, we'll create a roadmap to help you become eligible.

How long does the coaching program take?

The 3 coaching sessions are typically spread over 4-6 weeks, but you'll have ongoing email support throughout your entire application process.

Do you guarantee PR approval?

While we can't guarantee government decisions, our structured approach and expert guidance significantly increase your chances of success by avoiding common mistakes.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with the coaching program, we'll provide a full refund.
