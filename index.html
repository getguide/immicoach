Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our PR coaching program

What's the difference from a full‑representation service?

You maintain control and cost-effectiveness while getting expert-led guidance at each major milestone—no surprises, just results.

What if I'm not eligible for PR?

If you're not yet ready for Express Entry, we'll provide a personalized PR roadmap—and you'll only be charged for Session 1 ($235). The remaining balance will be refunded.

How long does the coaching program take?

The 3 coaching sessions are typically spread over 4-6 weeks, And you'll have ongoing email support throughout your entire coaching program.

Do you guarantee PR approval?

While no lawyer or consultant can promise an IRCC decision, our structured, expert-reviewed process helps avoid errors and delays that often derail applications.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes—if you're not satisfied after Session 1, you can request a refund within 10 days of your first session. A $150 administrative fee will be deducted to cover time, preparation, and resources. The remaining balance will be refunded to your original payment method.
